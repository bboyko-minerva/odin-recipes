Pizza

Description

Recipe for simple cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil (Optional)

Steps

  1. Make the dough, following a dough recipe
  2. Preheat oven
  3. Add sauce and then cheese to dough
  4. Cook for 10 minutes
  5. Let pizza sit for a few minutes before cutting it
  6. Serve with basil
